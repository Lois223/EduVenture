DESTINATIONS

Explore the captivating wonders of Ilocos Vigan, a destination steeped in history, culture, and natural beauty. From the iconic Bangui Windmills and majestic Kapurpurawan Rock Formation to the historic Malacañang of the North and the quaint charm of Calle Crisologo, each attraction offers a unique glimpse into the rich tapestry of Philippine heritage.

Immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring landscapes, from the rolling sand dunes of Paoay to the lush valleys of Pagudpud. Experience thrilling adventures like sandboarding or horseback riding, or simply bask in the tranquility of pristine beaches and verdant countryside.

Discover the vibrant local culture at bustling plazas, traditional pottery workshops, and wildlife sanctuaries like Baluarte Zoo. Indulge in luxurious accommodations at Fort Ilocandia Resort Hotel or savor the flavors of authentic Ilocano cuisine at local eateries.

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural enrichment, Ilocos Vigan offers a wealth of experiences to delight every traveler. Come and uncover the treasures of this enchanting destination, where the past meets the present in perfect harmony.

OUTDOOR BEAUTY

Bangui Windmills

The Bangui Windmills, located along the scenic coast of Bangui Bay in Ilocos Norte, stand as a testament to sustainable energy production and environmental stewardship. This pioneering wind farm consists of 20 colossal wind turbines that gracefully dot the shoreline, harnessing the power of the wind to generate clean, renewable electricity.

Kapurpurawan Rock Formation

Kapurpurawan Rock Formation is a stunning geological wonder located in Burgos, Ilocos Norte, Philippines. Carved by the relentless forces of wind and waves over millions of years, this natural marvel showcases the raw beauty and power of nature, offering visitors a glimpse into the Earth's geological history.

Patapat Viaduct

The Patapat Viaduct offers travelers a panoramic journey along one of the most scenic coastal routes in the Philippines. Perched on the edge of dramatic cliffs overlooking the azure waters of the West Philippine Sea, the viaduct provides unparalleled vistas of the coastline and surrounding mountains, making it a favorite stop for photographers and sightseers.

Paoay Sand Dunes

Paoay Sand Dunes, situated in the coastal town of Paoay in Ilocos Norte, Philippines, is a mesmerizing landscape of rolling sand hills and pristine beaches, offering visitors an exhilarating playground for adventure and exploration.

Fort Ilocandia Resort Hotel

Fort Ilocandia Resort Hotel, nestled amidst the lush landscapes of Ilocos Norte, offers guests a tranquil retreat where luxury meets natural beauty. Situated along the scenic coastline of Northern Luzon, this expansive resort is a haven for travelers seeking relaxation, recreation, and rejuvenation.

Calle Crisologo

Calle Crisologo, nestled in the heart of Vigan's historic district, is a charming thoroughfare that offers visitors a captivating journey back in time to the Spanish colonial era. Lined with well-preserved ancestral houses, cobblestone streets, and antique lampposts, this picturesque street is a living testament to Vigan's rich cultural heritage and architectural legacy.
